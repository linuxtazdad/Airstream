Travel Trailers / Corvallis, OR

REMODELED 1999 AIRSTREAM CLASSIC 31’

Our beautiful 1999 Airstream Classic Excella 1000 has been lovingly cared for and always stored inside. She was special ordered with two skylights, awnings all around, rock guards on the front and tinted vista window protectors, by her previous owners in 1998. A garage was built to await her arrival. She was taken out an average of twice a year to Airstream conventions, then washed and put away. Occasionally, they would simply sit in lawn chairs and gaze adoringly at her!
We bought her last year from this terrific couple. We have updated her interior to create a refreshing, elegant space. Our Classic Airstream has all new woodgrain vinyl plank flooring. The warm cream, beige, and gray lounge area is soothing and peaceful, inviting you to relax and unwind. It’s a great place to nestle into the soft cushions and pillows with a good book or to watch the 32” flat screen TV. The dining table and legs can be stowed away under the rear bench for an open, spacious floor plan. When the table is out, it provides a great place to dine or use your laptop. Additionally, the table can be placed down between the benches and a cushion slid over to create a large double bed. The back cushions on the long side of the lounge can also be removed for a quick comfortable single bed. The lounge has woven baskets under the bench providing an attractive storage space.
Our Airstream also comes with a Hensley Hitch to make the easy towing, even easier!
All the mechanical systems in our Airstream function well. The fridge works on both propane and electric, the stove top and oven operate like new, the microwave is amazing! (Seriously, I love this thing!) The new high arc, pull down faucet makes doing the dishes easier. The double porcelain sink and corian counters are fantastic. The kitchen has an abundance of storage with cabinets, drawers, overhead, and pantry pull outs. There is a huge closet in the hall and more drawers and cupboards. The bathroom, also sporting a corian countertop, has a new toilet, faucet, shower door and plenty of towel bars. The bedroom has a walk around queen bed with drawers on all sides and storage underneath. It has closets on both sides of the bed with wall sconces and overhead reading lights.
All of the windows operate smoothly. It is simple to get a nice breeze going through the Airstream on sunny days, but if that isn’t enough, she has a heat pump for cooling and heating that runs quietly and efficiently. There is also a propane furnace that keeps everything toasty if you don’t want to use the electric heat pump.
Or you want to Boondock! This is the camper you want for that. She is all set up for solar, with the controller installed. Large fresh, gray, and black holding tanks let you stay in the wilderness for close to 2 weeks! She even has her original awning blue airstream folding chairs in pristine condition! She does have a small ding behind her drivers side wheel, purely cosmetic.

Comes with: Hensley Hitch,new water pump, faucets, toilet, airstream factory replacement shower door, custom made J lounge, cushions, and dinette table w/stowable legs, woven storage baskets, vinyl plank flooring, smart tile backsplash in kitchen and bath, new towels bars and shelf in bath
